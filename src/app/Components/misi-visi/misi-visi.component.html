Here is a brief timeline of Stephan Hawkings life

  • 1942 - Stephen Hawking is born in Oxford, England.
  • 1959 - At 17 he began studying physics.
  • 1962 - Receives first-class honors degree.
  • 1963 - Meets his future wife.
  • 1963 - He is diagnosed with ALS just shy of 21.
  • 1965 - He marries his wife and has three children.
  • 1965 - He writes his thesis on space-time singularity.
  • 1969 - Health worsens and begins using crutches.
  • 1970 - He discovers hole dynamics
